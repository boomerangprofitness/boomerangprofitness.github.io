Boomerang Pro Fitness

At Boomerang Pro Fitness our aim is to bridge the gap between fitness assessment, and actual sporting performance. We provide comprehensive packages to provide athletes with what they need to achieve a competitive edge.

At an elite level the difference between winning and losing is separated by hundredths of seconds, so we consider every aspect of an athlete’s preparation to help them reach their maximum potential when and where it matters most.

We have experience working in the professional sporting arena. At Boomerang Pro Fitness we understand that the modern athlete has a team of technical coaches, conditioning coaches and sports therapists. As well as providing complete training solutions, we can add to this established dynamic by working with an athlete’s coaching team to provide real-time physiological analysis that can help with strategic and technical decision making, helping provide a truly competitive edge.
