Partners

Physique Management

Physique Management, formed in 1998, are a leading supplier of exceptional quality sports injury, sports massage and exercise products. Boomerang Pro Fitness work with Physique Management to make sure we use the best products in the field.

Polar

Listening to your body is one thing, understanding what it is telling you is an altogether different story. Polar helps you get under the skin of your training. They put it down to a combination of expertise in sports, physiology and electronics, coupled with a deep understanding of customer needs. It's no surprise then that they've been leading the way in technological innovations and heart rate monitors since 1977.

Position One Motorsports

Position One Motorsport provides market leading driver development services to the UK Motorsport industry. They can provide a full range of tailored services to the aspiring championship winning race driver, from trackside and in-car training through to simulator testing.

Science in Sport

We are proud to partner with industry leaders Science in Sport to help our customers get the right advice on tailoring their nutrition plans appropriately. Science in Sport is a leading sports nutrition company that develops, manufactures and markets sports nutrition products for professional athletes and sports enthusiasts. SiS is a strong brand in the elite athlete community - in the 2012 London Olympics, 24 medal-winning athletes or teams used SiS products.
